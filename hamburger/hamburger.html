Welcome, today you will learn how to make burgers!

To start, you must obtain all the ingredients listed:

  • buns
  • ground beef
  • ketchup
  • lettuce
  • cheese
  • pickles
  • tomatoes

Once you have your required ingredients, we can start cooking!

  1. slice your buns in half, and place them on the grill for 30 seconds
  2. measure 2 oz of beef and shape it into a circle
  3. place the patty on the grill for 5 minutes
  4. after 5 minutes, flip the patty and add on the cheese to the patty
  5. set up the burger
  6. place the lettuce first on the bun, then add the patty, then condiments
