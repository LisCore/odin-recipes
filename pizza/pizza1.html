Welcome, today, you learn how to make pizza!

Pizza is delicious and simple to make! First, you will need these ingredients.

  • dough
  • cheese
  • marinara sauce
  • an oven

Once these ingredients are prepped... Move on to the next steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 400 degrees Celcius.
  2. Roll your dough into a circle
  3. Place the sauce first
  4. Place about a cup of cheese onto the sauce, making sure it is covered evenly
  5. Place pepperoni
  6. Finally, place your pizza into the over for 40 minutes
